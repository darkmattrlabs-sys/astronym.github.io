2 About This Application

Cosmonym provides the following functionality:

  • Baby name suggestions, discovery, and inspiration tools
  • Astronomical data including star charts, constellation information, and celestial event tracking
  • Name-to-star or name-to-constellation associations and related features
  • Search, filtering, and favouriting functionality

The App is intended for general audiences including adults who are expecting parents or interested in astronomy. Cosmonym does not specifically target children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. See Section 11 for further details.
